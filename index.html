    Symptoms and Signs of having Arthritis

    Recognizing the Telltale Signs

    Joint Pain

    Experience persistent pain in one or more joints, worsening with movement.

    Joint Swelling

    visible swelling around affected joints, indicating inflammation.

    Morning Stiffness

    Feeling stiffness in the joints, particularly in the morning or after periods of inactivity.
